Research Data Collection Terms and Conditions

Introduction:

This document outlines the terms and conditions for participating in Passage Planner RoboCup Survey, conducted by DIAG departement of Sapienza University. By participating in this research, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

Participant Consent:

  1. By participating in this research study, you voluntarily agree to provide the requested information.
  2. Your participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.

Data Collection:

  1. We collect data for the sole purpose of training a robot policy for research purposes. This may include demographic information, ip address and survey responses.
  2. All collected data will be kept confidential and will only be used for the purpose of this research project.

Data Security:

  1. We take measures to protect the security and confidentiality of your data. Access to collected data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  2. Collected data will be stored securely in our servers and will be retained for 12 months after the completion of the research.

Data Usage:

  1. Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for academic publications, presentations, or other research-related purposes. Individual participants will not be personally identifiable in any such publication.
  2. Your personal information will not be shared with any third parties unless required by law.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about this research or your participation, you may contact Vincenzo Suriani suriani@diag.uniroma1.it.

Changes to Terms and Conditions:

These terms and conditions are subject to change. Any updates will be communicated to participants.

By participating in this research, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these terms and conditions and agree to comply with them.

Thank you for your participation!
